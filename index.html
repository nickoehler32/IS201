Four keys to
a healthy lifestyle!

Keep a daily journal

Listing down some of your thoughts and experiences throughout each day is a great way to keep an open mind.

Exercise regularly

Aim for AT LEAST 30 minutes of exercise daily. Whether in the gym or at home, a healthy heart will lead to a healthy mind!

Adequate sleep

Sleep is extremely important when it comes to a healthy lifestyle. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night.

Keep a healthy diet

Eat a variety of foods from all food groups. Drink plenty of fluids to remain hydrated!
